Our Story

Our Story – Kumanayaka Food Center

Kumanayaka Food Center, which was founded in 2025, was born from a dream to serve the city of Vientiane as a place to enjoy delicious, quality food. What started as a small family project has today become one of the most popular food destinations in the city.

Our journey began with the passion of our Head Chef Udayapala Kumanayaka. A lover of home-cooked recipes learned from his mother, he decided to share that taste with everyone. Together with his assistant Chandrani, they created an establishment that “doesn’t just eat – life itself is considered a celebration”.

Today, Kumanayaka Food Center is an establishment that combines traditional Sri Lankan and modern food concepts. We work with faith and love in our cooking methods, using ingredients mostly from local farmers and trusted suppliers. Through this, we present each gift to you in a delicious and healthy way.

Kumanayaka Food Center – A place where delicious memories are made.
